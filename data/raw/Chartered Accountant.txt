1. Chartered Accountant (CA)
Role Summary:
A Chartered Accountant is responsible for managing accounting, auditing, taxation, and 
financial advisory services while ensuring compliance with statutory and regulatory 
requirements.
Key Responsibilities:
• Prepare and review financial statements as per applicable accounting standards
• Manage direct and indirect tax compliance (Income Tax, GST, TDS)
• Conduct audits and provide assurance on financial records
• Advise management on financial planning, budgeting, and cost control
• Ensure compliance with Companies Act, Income Tax Act, and GST laws
• Support business decision-making through financial analysis
Skills & Qualifications:
• Qualified Chartered Accountant (ICAI)
• Strong knowledge of accounting standards, taxation, and audit
• Analytical and problem-solving skills
• Proficiency in accounting software and Excel
2. Practicing Chartered Accountant
Role Summary:
A Practicing CA provides professional services independently or through a firm, offering 
audit, taxation, compliance, and advisory services to multiple clients.
Key Responsibilities:
• Handle statutory, tax, and internal audits for clients
• File income tax and GST returns for individuals and businesses
• Provide business, financial, and compliance advisory services
• Represent clients before tax authorities and regulators
• Manage client relationships and ensure service quality
• Stay updated with regulatory and legal changes
Skills & Qualifications:
• CA qualification with Certificate of Practice (COP)
• Client handling and communication skills
• Strong knowledge of laws and regulations
• Business development and advisory capability

A Compliance Consultant ensures businesses meet statutory, regulatory, and governance 
requirements through structured compliance management.
Key Responsibilities
• Manage ROC, MCA, labor law, and tax compliances
• Develop compliance calendars and tracking systems
• Assist clients during audits and due diligence
• Ensure timely filings and documentation
• Advise on regulatory changes and impact
• Coordinate with legal, HR, and finance teams
Skills & Qualifications
• CA / CS with compliance expertise
• Strong regulatory and documentation skills
• Ability to manage multiple clients
Career Scope
• Compliance Practice Owner
• Risk & Governance Consultant
107. Owner / Managing Partner – CA Firm
Role Overview
The Owner / Managing Partner of a CA Firm is responsible for leading a full-service 
professional practice delivering audit, taxation, compliance, and advisory services while 
ensuring regulatory compliance and business growth.
Key Responsibilities
• Establish and manage CA practice in line with ICAI regulations
• Deliver services including audit, taxation, accounting, and advisory
• Build and manage a diversified client portfolio
• Oversee firm operations, staffing, training, and quality control
• Ensure ethical standards, peer review, and compliance requirements
• Develop business development strategies and partnerships
• Represent clients before tax and regulatory authorities
Skills & Qualifications
• Qualified CA with Certificate of Practice (COP)
• Strong technical knowledge and leadership skills
• Client acquisition and relationship management capability
Business Scope
• Sole proprietorship or partnership firm
• Multi-disciplinary professional services firm
108. Founder – Tax Consultancy Firm
Role Overview
The Founder of a Tax Consultancy Firm specializes in direct and indirect tax advisory, 
compliance, and litigation services for individuals, SMEs, and corporates.
Key Responsibilities
• Provide income tax, corporate tax, and GST advisory services
• Handle tax planning, assessments, audits, and litigation
• Manage return filings and statutory compliance
• Represent clients before tax authorities and appellate forums
• Build tax expertise teams and knowledge frameworks
• Stay updated with tax law changes and judicial precedents
Skills & Qualifications
• CA with strong taxation specialization
• Litigation and advisory experience preferred
• Client advisory and documentation skills
Business Scope
• Individual & corporate tax advisory
• High-value tax planning & representation services
109. Founder / Partner – Audit & Assurance Firm
Role Overview
The Founder / Partner of an Audit & Assurance Firm provides independent audit and 
assurance services to ensure financial transparency, governance, and regulatory compliance.
Key Responsibilities
• Conduct statutory, tax, internal, and concurrent audits
• Lead audit planning, execution, and reporting
• Ensure compliance with auditing standards and ICAI guidelines
• Build sector-specific audit expertise (banking, manufacturing, NGOs)
• Maintain audit quality, documentation, and peer review readiness
• Develop long-term assurance relationships with clients
Skills & Qualifications
• CA with audit and assurance experience
• Strong understanding of auditing standards and risk assessment
• High ethical standards and professional judgment
Business Scope
• Statutory & internal audit services
• Specialized audits (bank, forensic, system audits)
110. Founder – GST & Compliance Firm
Role Overview
The Founder of a GST & Compliance Firm focuses on managing indirect tax compliance and 
regulatory obligations for businesses across industries.
Key Responsibilities
• Provide GST registration, return filing, and advisory services
• Handle GST reconciliations, audits, and departmental notices
• Manage ROC, MCA, labor law, and other statutory compliances
• Develop compliance calendars and automation processes
• Support startups and SMEs with end-to-end compliance solutions
• Train clients and internal teams on compliance best practices
Skills & Qualifications
• CA with strong GST and regulatory knowledge
• Process orientation and compliance management expertise
Business Scope
• Retainer-based compliance services
• Compliance outsourcing for startups and MSMEs

Business Scope
• Transaction advisory & investment consulting
• High-value strategic finance engagements
112. Founder – Virtual CFO Services Company
Role Overview
The Founder of a Virtual CFO Services Company delivers CFO-level strategic and 
operational finance services to startups and SMEs on a fractional or retainer basis.
Key Responsibilities
• Provide budgeting, forecasting, and cash flow management
• Monitor burn rate, runway, and unit economics
• Support fundraising, investor reporting, and board discussions
• Implement MIS, dashboards, and financial controls
• Oversee accounting, taxation, and compliance teams
• Act as strategic finance partner to founders and CEOs
Skills & Qualifications
• CA with startup, SME, or corporate finance experience
• Strong strategic thinking and communication skills
Business Scope
• Retainer-based Virtual CFO engagements
• Scalable finance outsourcing model
